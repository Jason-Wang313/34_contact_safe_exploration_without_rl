# Novelty Decision

Decision: proceed as a mechanism paper.

The strongest version is a certificate-growing view of robot contact exploration. The paper should concede that safe exploration and barrier certificates are mature areas. Its useful contribution is to move contact exploration away from reward learning and toward guarded physical expansion of what the robot is allowed to touch next.

V2 boundary: proceed only as workshop-only / strong-revise. The discontinuity stress shows that certificate expansion depends on calibrated local smoothness and violation harm; conservative probing can be better when brittle low-limit pockets are common.
